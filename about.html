About MKL Tech

Started in Cherry Hill, NJ — built for people who just want their computers to work.

MKL Tech started because we noticed a clear gap in our community: people were overpaying for computers that didn’t match their needs. Big-box stores pushed unnecessary parts, online guides were confusing, and trustworthy alternatives were hard to find.

We began by helping classmates and local families build and repair PCs that fit real workloads and budgets. Over time, that work grew into MKL Tech, a small, focused PC service built on transparent pricing, clear explanations, and reliable performance.

Today, MKL Tech focuses on students, families, and budget-limited users who need reliable computers without getting lost in technical jargon or pressured into overspending.
